20.05.2022

Notes:

  • We started model training.

  • At the same time, we are preparing an additional hardware. This hardware allows you to rotate and move around the game. We shared the first working prototype with Prof.

  • we will solder the circuit, put it in a box that we got from the 3D printing, and sew it into a glove. In this way, the user will be able to move around the virtual environment without using a keyboard and mouse. It will interact with objects such as doors, lights by thinking.

24.04.2022

Notes:

  • After our hardware materials arrived, the operation of the sensors was tested.

  • Incoming data was examined.

  • What can be done on the meaning of the data was discussed.

  • The bluetooth connection of the rasberry and the headband has been studied and the connection has been established.

  • We started doing the demo via Unity.

  • Had a discussion on having a piece of hardware and whether building it or buying it off the rack.
